Neural Machine Translation Datasets

Aligned datasets mentioned in the paper Evaluating Neural Machine Translation in the Medical Domain (WWW2019) can be downloaded below:

  1. EXPERT fully aligned dataset: A medical-expert filtered corpus of fully aligned Wikipedia-Simple Wikipedia sentences in English.
  2. EXPERT partially aligned dataset: A medical-expert filtered corpus of partially aligned Wikipedia-Simple Wikipedia sentences in English.
  3. AUTOMATED dataset: A BLEU-aligned corpus of aligned Wikipedia-Simple Wikipedia sentences in English.
